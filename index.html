Rise to Another World: Hero's Awakening

Written by Jade Nguyen and Brandon Xiong

Welcome readers! Our story: 'Rise to Another World: Hero's Awakening' is about a character who returns their home to rest, only to wake up entangled in a fantasy world. Within this new world, you will be playing as the main character, exploring and engaging in particular action. As the character, you will heed your fate as an uprising hero.
